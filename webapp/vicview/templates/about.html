Our Mission

Regional Institutions offer a wide variety of courses in different disciplines, however, due to their remote location, there is an assumption that these institutes do not offer proper amenities for students, and living conditions in those areas would be sub-standard as compared to those available in metropolitan regions. In reality, some suburbs in regional Victoria have excellent facilities for students, which go unused every year. VicView was developed We want to help more students know about regional Institutions, and the courses they offer. We also want to assist them in finding ideal suburbs that they can call home. This will spread awareness among year 12 students in metropolitan areas, of the many options available to them in the future, and will subsequently lead to the upliftment of regional areas.


What does VicView do?

  • Users can Look up Professions and find important details like Average wage, Weekly work hours, Future trends, etc.
  • Users can find all the Institutions offering a specific course, and details about the Institute.
  • Users can find ideal suburbs around the Institute and even compare various aspects about them.
